Welcome Cocktail

The welcome cocktail will take place on Monday at Iglesia Mozárabe de San Sebastián.
This Mudejar church in Toledo showcases an evolution from the 10th to the 20th century, built on an Islamic temple with Visigothic elements like carved capitals. Following restoration, features of its Islamic phase, like the horseshoe arch and intricate north wall, are visible, along with later handmade details from 1916. Together, these elements reflect Toledo's blend of historical styles and cultures, embodying the city's unique heritage.

Social Dinner

The conference dinner will take place at Cigarral del Angel.
El Cigarral del Ángel Custodio is an estate of over 100,000 square meters, divided into two areas: Palacete de Fina and Zona del Río. It is renowned for its incredible views, and spectacular gardens. This is the only Michelin restaurant in Toldedo with menus crafted by the chef Iván Cerdeño.
We will provide a shuttle from and to the conference venue.
