Welcome Cocktail

The welcome cocktail will take place at Museo de Santa Cruz .
The Museo de Santa Cruz in Toledo is a 15th-century building that was originally a hospital. It houses an impressive collection of art, archaeology, and ceramics. Highlights include works by El Greco and medieval and Renaissance pieces.

Conference Dinner

The conference dinner will take place at Cigarral del Angel.
El Cigarral del Ángel Custodio is an estate of over 100,000 square meters, divided into two areas: Palacete de Fina and Zona del Río. It is renowned for its incredible views, and spectacular gardens. This is the only Michelin restaurant in Toldedo with menus crafted by the chef Iván Cerdeño.
We will provide a shuttle from and to Plaza Zocodover, 200 meters away from the conference venue.
